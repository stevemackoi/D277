Introduction to California

California, dude, it's like this epic state on the West Coast that goes all the way from the Mexican border up along the Pacific for almost 900 miles. You've got these gnarly beaches with cliffs, towering redwood forests, the massive Sierra Nevada Mountains, endless Central Valley farmland, and even the scorching Mojave Desert. Totally rad, right?
